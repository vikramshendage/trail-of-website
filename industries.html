Industries Served

Powering critical infrastructure and industrial operations across global sectors

Applications

Engineered for Demanding Environments

Different industries present unique electrical, environmental, and thermal challenges. We design custom transformers tailored to these specific needs.

Power Utilities

Transmission and distribution grids require high-efficiency power transformers with low core losses, tap changers, and robust short-circuit withstand capabilities.

Solar Power Plants

Solar inverter duty transformers support multiple split secondaries to handle inverter harmonics and withstand severe daily load cycling.

Wind Farms

Designed to fit inside nacelles or tower bases, these units feature compact footprints, low flammability ester fluid, and resistance to high-frequency transients.

Data Centers

Continuous uptime demands ultra-reliable transformers. We supply dry-type VPI and Cast Resin transformers featuring low noise, low losses, and zero fire risk.

Railways & Transit

Traction substations depend on heavy-duty traction and isolation transformers to manage rapid load changes and high harmonic levels.

Mining & Extraction

Underground and surface mines present extreme vibration, dust, and moisture. Our rugged, encapsulated transformers operate reliably in these settings.

Oil & Gas

Offshore platforms and refineries require explosion-proof, corrosion-resistant transformers utilizing synthetic esters and specialized hermetic enclosures.

Hospitals & Commercial

Where human safety and silence are top priorities. Our dry-type insulation prevents toxic gas emission and minimizes acoustic hum in indoor rooms.

Steel & Metallurgy

Furnace and rectifier duty transformers configured to handle high thermal stresses, frequent short-circuit events, and massive low-voltage current output.
