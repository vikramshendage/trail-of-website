PAGE 02 // POWER SOLUTIONS

High Voltage Power Solutions

SKA Power Transformers are custom-engineered for heavy-duty transmission networks, power generation facilities, and major industrial substations.

Designed to handle bulk electrical energy transfer with minimal load losses, these oil-immersed units feature ratings ranging from 10 MVA up to 150 MVA with primary voltage ratings up to 220 kV class. Each transformer is designed using 3D Electromagnetic Finite Element Method (FEM) simulation software to withstand severe dynamic short-circuit stress, thermal overloads, and seismic acceleration forces.

Insulation media options include premium mineral oil or high fire-point natural ester fluids (FR3), paired with automated cooling arrangements (ONAN / ONAF / OFAF / ODAF) for uncompromised grid availability.

PAGE 05 // CONSTRUCTION DETAILS

Construction Details & Active Part Assembly

1. Core & Clamping Assembly

Fabricated from prime grade, Hi-B CRGO silicon steel laminations with step-lap mitered joints. Limbs are clamped under controlled hydraulic pressure using resin-bonded glass bands to ensure minimal excitation current and low sound levels.

2. CTC Windings & Hydraulic Sizing

Windings utilize Continuously Transposed Conductors (CTC) of electrolytic copper. Disc and helical coil structures feature radial pressboard spacers. Coils are thermally processed and clamped under a 120-ton hydraulic press to guarantee short-circuit strength.

3. Vapour Phase Drying (VPD) & Tanking

The active part is dried inside a MICAFIL Vapour Phase Drying plant to reduce insulation moisture below 0.5%. The core-coil assembly is locked into the heavy steel tank and oil-impregnated under deep vacuum.

PAGE 06 // TESTING & QA

High Voltage Factory Testing

Every power transformer is subjected to complete routine, type, and special tests per IEEE C57.12.90 & CSA C88 standards in our high-voltage test laboratory.

Routine Tests
  • Winding Resistance on all tap positions
  • Voltage Ratio, Polarity & Phase Relation
  • No-Load Loss & Excitation Current
  • Load Loss & Impedance Voltage
  • Separate-Source Withstand AC Voltage
  • Induced Overvoltage Withstand Test
  • Insulation Resistance & Dissipation Factor (Tan Delta)
Type & Special Tests
  • Temperature Rise Test at full rating
  • Lightning Impulse Voltage Test (Full & Chopped Wave)
  • Switching Impulse Test
  • Partial Discharge Measurement (<50 pC)
  • Sweep Frequency Response Analysis (SFRA)
  • Sound Level Test per NEMA TR-1
