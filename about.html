Who We Are

3.5 Decades of Engineering Innovation

SKA Transformers has evolved from a regional manufacturer into a trusted multinational provider of critical electrical infrastructure.

Our journey began with a vision to deliver robust, high-efficiency electrical equipment. Today, we specialize in high-voltage design, simulation-driven development, and custom transformer engineering.

Serving utilities, industrial networks, and renewable projects globally, we build transformers that comply with IEEE, UL, CSA, and IEC standards, ensuring absolute safety and performance.

  • Customer-First Approach: Long-term partnerships over short-term gains.

Operations & Quality Excellence

Engineering & Manufacturing

Our 10,000+ Sq. Meters facility is equipped with advanced automated winding machines and vacuum drying ovens. From rigorous Design Engineering to exact Tank Fabrication, every step ensures a robust final product.

Quality Commitment

We guarantee 100% Routine Lab Testing on all units. For new product designs, we strictly adhere to CSA, CAN, and IEEE standards, ensuring unparalleled Customer Satisfaction and continuous improvement.

Research & Development

Our engineering division utilizes state-of-the-art 3D FEM Electromagnetic Simulation to analyze thermal behavior and short-circuit forces prior to production, driving constant Technical Development.
