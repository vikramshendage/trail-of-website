What is a VPI Transformer?

Safety and Compliance Under One Design

A Vacuum Pressure Impregnation (VPI) Transformer is an advanced dry-type, air-cooled transformer that uses ambient air ventilation instead of liquid coolants. This eliminates the risk of oil leaks, ground contamination, and fire hazards, making it the safest choice for densely populated or environmentally sensitive areas.

Operating Principle: Unlike oil-filled variants, the heat generated by the core and windings is dissipated directly into the surrounding air through natural convection (AN) or forced air (AF). The VPI process ensures the windings are deeply impregnated with a high-temperature polyester resin, providing exceptional mechanical strength and dielectric integrity.

Differences from Oil-Filled Transformers:
  • No Fire Hazard: Self-extinguishing materials; no flammable oils.
  • Eco-Friendly: Zero risk of toxic leaks or soil contamination.
  • Indoor Safe: Can be installed close to the load in basements or high-rises.
  • Maintenance Free: No oil sampling, filtering, or level checking required.

Construction Details

Advanced HV Windings & Comb Design

SKA designed HV windings utilize custom-fabricated transformer winding combs made from Fiber Glass Reinforced Plastic Grating (GP03) insulation material. Comb designs are explicitly tailored for windings, focusing on creating high, even tensions. This facilitates rapid sectioning and superior heat management.

Key Innovations
  • Specialized carbon fiber tail combs with gradually narrowing teeth.
  • Long, thin tails designed for rapid manual manipulations.
  • V-shaped foldable combs ensuring controlled and consistent curling.

High-grade, cold-rolled, grain-oriented (CRGO) silicon steel stacked using a step-lap method to reduce core losses and noise.

Wound using continuous copper or aluminum conductors insulated with Class 220 materials like Nomex or fiberglass.

Typically uses foil winding technology to balance axial short-circuit forces and provide superior thermal dissipation.

Windings are subjected to alternating cycles of vacuum and pressure while submerged in high-temperature polyester resin, followed by oven curing.

Compliant with NEMA 3R and rigorously tested as per C57.12.91. Features S.S. 304 M8 countersunk bolts, 7-tank process surface treatment, 25x3 adhesive foam gaskets, and premium ANSI 61 GREY paint. Enclosure panels are completely detachable for maintenance.

Cooling System

Our transformers are designed with highly efficient thermal dissipation systems ensuring reliable long-term performance.

  • AN (Air Natural): Standard base rating relying on natural convective airflow through the enclosure louvers and winding ducts.
  • AF (Air Forced): Integrated cooling fans automatically trigger based on load temperatures, providing a capacity boost of up to 33% beyond the base AN rating.

Protection & Monitoring

Advanced digital monitoring ensures long-term operational health and safety integration.

  • RTD Sensors & PT100: Embedded directly into the LV windings for precise thermal monitoring.
  • Digital Temperature Indicator: Real-time display with programmable setpoints.
  • Alarm & Trip Contacts: Pre-configured thresholds to alert operators or trip incoming breakers before thermal damage occurs.
  • Cooling Fan Controller: Automated operation based on sensor feedback.
  • Space Heater & SCADA: Prevents condensation during off-cycles; compatible with RS-485 / Modbus thermal protection systems.
