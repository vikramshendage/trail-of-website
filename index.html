R&D CAPABILITIES

Innovation & Advanced Engineering

Leveraging cutting-edge technology to build smarter, greener, and more resilient transformers.

3D Electromagnetic Simulation

Our design engineering team utilizes advanced finite element analysis (FEA) to simulate electromagnetic fields, thermal hotspots, and mechanical stress. This ensures absolute precision in core-coil sizing and loss minimization prior to physical manufacturing.

Eco-Friendly Ester Fluids

We pioneer the integration of natural and synthetic ester dielectric fluids (KNAN/KNAF). These bio-degradable fluids drastically reduce environmental impact, completely eliminate fire hazards (K-class), and allow for higher overload capacities.
